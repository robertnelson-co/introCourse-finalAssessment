Horatio

Adoption date: 3 July 2003

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

Horatio was a very obedient and docile dog. He was 17 when he passed away, but from puppyhood he matched the energy of his family perfectly. Here are some quick facts about him:

  1. He lived in three different states: Maryland, Virginia, and North Carolina.
  2. Horatio loved peanut butter and gravy mixed in with his food.
  3. Never, even once in his life, did he successfully play fetch - or swim!
